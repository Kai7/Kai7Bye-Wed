Part 1: Application region

first, the leftmost icon is our menu. After clicking the icon, it will show the application installing on this computer.

The other icons are file-management, iceweasel, lxterminal, and showdesktop respectively.

file-management

User directory, also known as the D:\\ in Microsoft Windows. All of your data will put in this space.

iceweasel

A kind of browser which is brother of molliza firefox. It is very very similar to firefox and useful.

lxterminal

Just a space for you to command.

Part 4: System region

The left icon is ScreenLock. Something like logout, but you are still online. If you want to use the system, it require keying in your password again.

The right icon is Logout, but its function is not just logout, ex: shutdown, reboot ... etc. So you must be careful.

The function we need is the second-last button, logout, because our computer has no use for shutdown.
